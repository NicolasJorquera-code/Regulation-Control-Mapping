# Risk Inventory Demo Executive Scenarios

## Walkthrough

1. Open Risk Inventory Builder and enable Demo Mode.
2. In Knowledge Base, choose `Large Global Bank`, `Local Regional Bank`, or `Digital Payments Institution`.
3. Review profile complexity cards and the Business Unit Risk Capture Matrix.
4. Use the scope selector to drill from all BUs into a BU or process.
5. Open Risk Inventory, Control Mapping, Gap Analysis, Review & Challenge, and Executive Report without adding any new top-level tabs.

## What Executives Should Notice

- Payment Operations captures operational and data risks through exception, reconciliation, control, issue, evidence, and KRI packs.
- Retail Banking & Onboarding captures compliance, privacy, fraud, and dispute risk through onboarding/dispute evidence and regulatory obligations.
- Commercial Lending captures model, covenant, contract, and documentation risks through credit workflow evidence.
- Treasury & Liquidity captures resiliency, reporting, settlement, collateral, and source-data risks.
- Technology & Cyber Operations captures privileged access, vulnerability remediation, change, and cyber evidence aging.

## Demo Positioning

The Knowledge Base tab is intentionally elevated as the modularity showcase. Risk Inventory remains the record-level scoring and drill-down workbench.
