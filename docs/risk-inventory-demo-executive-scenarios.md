# Risk Inventory Demo Executive Scenarios

## Walkthrough

1. Open Risk Inventory Builder and enable Demo Mode.
2. The demo opens `Payment Exception Handling` for the `Payment Operations` business unit automatically.
3. In Knowledge Base, review the process metadata, source packs, and public scenario-basis trace.
4. Open Risk Inventory, Control Mapping, and Gap Analysis to review the risk records, mapped controls, KRIs, evidence, issues, and action plans.
5. Export the workbook to inspect the same focused scenario with reviewer decisions and source trace.

## What Executives Should Notice

- Payment Operations captures payment-release, maker/checker, reconciliation, recall, escalation, privileged-access, and data-quality risks through a single end-to-end exception-handling process.
- The scenario is grounded in public erroneous wire-transfer litigation summaries, payment-system supervisory guidance, and current-period control evidence.
- The workbench shows how one realistic process can carry multiple risk records, controls, KRIs, evidence artifacts, issues, appetite thresholds, validation findings, and remediation plans.

## Demo Positioning

The Knowledge Base tab is intentionally elevated as the traceability showcase. Risk Inventory remains the record-level scoring and drill-down workbench.
